FOUNDER & DATA Science Director

Paula M. Petrone, PhD

Paula Petrone has a decade's experience implementing novel technology and analytics to support decision-making in the life science industry. PhD in Biophysics from Stanford University, her field of expertise is the integration and mining of heterogeneous information at the intersection of chemistry, biology and medicine.

Dr, Petrone serves as a data science advisor helping startups and Pharma companies with their digital strategy and innovation programs. She is a firm advocate of diversity in the STEM careers. She is an author of several scientific publications, active public speaker, mentor, and mother of two children.
